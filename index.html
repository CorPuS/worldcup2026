2026 World Cup Prediction Game

Each player predicts the final score of the matches in the tournament by entering the expected number of goals for both teams.

After each match, the results are updated and points are automatically calculated.

Each player sees their own points and can view other players' predictions.

Predictions can be submitted no later than 1 hour before kickoff.

Points Calculation

2 points – maximum amount for one match
  • 0.5 points – for correctly guessing the winner
  • up to 0.5 points – for correctly guessing the goal difference (if winner was also guessed correctly)
  • up to 0.5 points – first team's goal count
  • up to 0.5 points – second team's goal count

Exactly guessing the result earns 2 points. Each deviation from the actual result reduces the point count.

You can use the points counter on the right to predict the points for your guess before submitting it.

Due to rounding, there might be some discrepancies in the points calculation (up to 0.01 points).
